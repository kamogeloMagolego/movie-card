Avengers : Infinity War

About the Movie

As the Avengers and their allies have continued to protect the world from challenges too great for any one hero to bear, a new enemy has emerged from the cosmic shadows: Thanos. His objective as a cosmic ruler is to collect all six Infinity Stones, objects of immeasurable power, and use them to impose his twisted will on all of reality. Everything the Avengers have fought for has culminated in this moment, when the fate of Earth and life itself has never been more unclear.
